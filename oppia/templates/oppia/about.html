Health Training Schools in Ghana

ME4Health (Mobile Education for Health) has been developed with the support of Maternal and Child Survival Programme (MCSP), Jhpiego and the Ministry of Health for Health Training Schools in Ghana. ME4Health application is developed with the aim of providing learners access to evidence based health resources.

OppiaMobile is the mobile learning platform from Digital Campus to deliver learning content, multimedia and quizzes on your smartphone. All the content and activities can be accessed and used even when you don't have an internet connection available on your mobile.

To get started with our courses, download the OppiaMobile app. {% endblocktrans %}
